Dataset Name: Adult Income Dataset (also known as Census Income)
Source: UCI Machine Learning Repository
Download Link: https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/adult
Description: 
The dataset consists of 48,842 instances with 14 attributes (age, workclass, education, marital-status, occupation, etc.).
The goal is to predict whether an individual's income exceeds $50K/year based on census data.
